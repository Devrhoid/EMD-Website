Site Tutorial

Home page:
You may interact with the map by left clicking in the map area and dragging to the location you desire. You may also use the slider to the left to zoom to a particular location or use a mouse to zoom. The direction pad located at the top left of the map area may be used to move the map in different directions as well. You may switch between the roadmap and satellite view by clicking the appropriate tab in the top right of the map area. Use the tabs to navigate to different pages of the site as required. You may get additional information on the markers by clicking on each one. If you have any additional questions, please send us an email at support@uwiremotedata.com.
